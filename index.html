About DarkNyteGPT

In the rapidly evolving landscape of cyber threats, passive defense is no longer enough. DarkNyteGPT harnesses the power of advanced LLMs and specialized machine learning models to provide security intelligence that is both fast and deeply contextual.

Our core mission is to democratize security. By building this framework as an open-source tool, we empower security teams, independent researchers, and developers worldwide to strengthen their defenses and understand the adversary's perspective through ethical simulation and analysis.
